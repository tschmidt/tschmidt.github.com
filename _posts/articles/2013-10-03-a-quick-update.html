So, my goal of publishing articles on a regular basis completely failed. But, there is a reason for that. Over the past year, I started work on our paperless admissions web application, created some new tools to help me with my development work and received 2 pretty amazing awards.

This year has been amazing so far and I'm really looking forward to what is still coming.
